Our Story

TimeCraft started as a simple idea: what if a timer website was actually a pleasure to use? Most timer tools online are cluttered with intrusive ads, require sign-ups, load slowly, and look like they haven't been updated since 2010.

We believe that a free utility should still be well-designed, fast, and respectful of your time and attention. TimeCraft loads instantly, works offline after first load, and gives you every time tool you need in one beautiful interface.

Whether you're using a 25-minute Pomodoro timer for focused work, tracking your morning run with the stopwatch, or checking what time it is in Tokyo before scheduling a meeting — TimeCraft has you covered.
