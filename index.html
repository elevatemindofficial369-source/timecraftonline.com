FAQ

Is TimeCraft completely free?
Yes! TimeCraft is 100% free to use. All features — timer, alarm, stopwatch, and world clock — are available without any subscription or sign-up.
Do I need to install anything?
No installation needed. TimeCraft runs entirely in your browser. Just open the page and start using it immediately on any device — desktop, tablet, or mobile.
How does the alarm clock work?
Enter your desired alarm time in the Alarm tab and click Set Alarm. TimeCraft checks the time every few seconds and plays a beep sound when your alarm time arrives. Keep the browser tab open for the alarm to work.
What is a Pomodoro timer?
The Pomodoro technique uses 25-minute focused work sessions followed by a 5-minute break. Use the 25 min quick button for a perfect Pomodoro timer. After 4 sessions, take a longer 15-30 minute break.
Does the timer work when I switch tabs?
Yes. The timer runs in the background even when you switch to another tab. The audio alert will still play when time is up, as long as the browser tab remains open.
