🍅 What is the Pomodoro Technique?

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method developed by Francesco Cirillo in the 1980s. It uses a timer to break work into intervals, traditionally 25 minutes in length, separated by short breaks.

How to Use Pomodoro Technique

  1. Choose a task you want to work on
  2. Set the timer for 25 minutes (1 Pomodoro)
  3. Work on the task with full focus until the timer rings
  4. Take a 5-minute short break
  5. After completing 4 Pomodoros, take a longer break (15–30 minutes)

Benefits

  • Boosts concentration and focus
  • Reduces mental fatigue and burnout
  • Helps overcome procrastination
  • Increases productivity significantly
